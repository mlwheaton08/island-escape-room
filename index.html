OUR STORY

Forrest Palis started this one-man project in the winter of 2023. After visiting other escape rooms, he was inpired to build his own. He started very small, crafting an escape room in his own house for his family to test out. Witnessing the amount of fun they had gave him all the confidence he needed to rent a space and go all-in on creating his own professional escape room. He worked tirelessly outside of his day job, learning carpentry skills and building a room all on his own. He studied puzzles, took notes, and came up with his own perfectly themed puzzles to give the customers a once-in-a-lifetime experience. After running test groups with friends, family, and strangers, and tweaking a few things here and there, he officially opened the first escape room at The Island, and currently the only escape room in Carbondale, in November 2024. With the business up and running, Forrest has begun planning his next escape room, Museum Heist.
